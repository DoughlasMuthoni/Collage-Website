Constitution of Board Of Governors

As per the TVET Act 2024, the Board Of Governors consists of:

  1. A chairperson who shall be appointed by Cabinet Secretary Ministry Of education.
  2. The Principal Secretary, the State Department for TVET or designated representative.
  3. The Principal Secretary responsible for finance or designated representative.
  4. A representative nominated by the Kenya Engineering Technology Registration Board.
  5. One principal representing TVET institutions.
  6. Three independent members appointed by the Cabinet Secretary in any of the following areas:
    • Leadership and management
    • Financial management
    • Technology
    • Industry
    • Engineering
    • Information communication technology
  7. The Chief Executive Officer of the School.
